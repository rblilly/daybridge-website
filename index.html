How we care

Whole family care.

A day program built for people living with memory loss — and those who care for them.

Daytime connection

Familiar faces, consistent routines, and compassionate staff create days filled with joy, purpose, and real connection.

Small groups. Tailored programming that supports memory, mobility, and emotional wellbeing.

Nutritious meals. Freshly prepared hot lunch, healthy snacks, and a focus on hydration.

1:6 direct care ratio. A safe, structured environment staffed by dementia-trained professionals.

A true clinical partner

Our interdisciplinary team pairs personalized daytime programming with clinical support that evolves as needs change.

Medication support. Overseen by RNs, aligned with provider-prescribed care plans.

Therapy onsite. Physical, occupational, and mental health, prescribed by your provider.

Earlier visibility. Earlier visibility. Daily check-ins, monthly vitals, and quarterly assessments to surface changes early.

For caregivers, too

Designed to support the entire family, not just their loved one. We equip caregivers with the time, tools, and support they deserve.

Reliable respite. Time every week to work, rest, or simply breathe.

Support for home. Training and resources to strengthen care outside our center.

Caregiver community. Counseling and support groups, available at every step.
