About us

We saw what was missing, so we built it.

If you're here, you already know how hard this is. A diagnosis that raises more questions than it answers. A healthcare system that treats and manages dementia but doesn't support the daily reality of living with it. The exhausting work of piecing everything together yourself while trying to explain it to anyone who hasn't lived it.

For the past decade, we've led care teams serving hundreds of thousands of older adults in primary care at Oak Street Health and specialty care at DaVita Kidney Care. Again and again, we saw the same story: families navigating dementia with far too little support. We knew families deserved better.

We set out to build what was missing.

Over the past 18 months, we've worked alongside clinical advisors from leading Chicago-area and national health systems, geriatric practices, and neurology groups with one goal: to improve life for people living with memory loss and the families who care for them. Daybridge is our answer.

You and your family deserve a real place to turn. We built Daybridge so you don't have to carry this alone, and we'd be honored to be part of your journey.
