A day at Daybridge

Joy, purpose, and connection, every day.

Here's a glimpse of what members can expect.

08:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Arrival & Check-In
Members arrive in small groups to ease the morning transition. Daily health screenings are completed upon arrival, while members settle in and enjoy coffee or tea together.
10:00 AM – 11:00 AM
Enrichment Activities
Cognitive activities like memory games and trivia are scheduled early in the day, when minds are freshest and alert. Members choose among options that match their interests. Our trained staff makes sure every activity is adapted to meet each member's needs.
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Movement
We prioritize movement that incorporates dual-task activities — exercises that engage mind and body. Our approach improves balance, reduces falls, and supports overall function. Activities include tai chi, ballroom dancing, walking conversations, and chair exercises, all adapted to each member's abilities.
12:00 PM – 01:00 PM
Lunch
Lunch is served communally, with space for small groups to break off. Shared meals naturally encourage conversation, connection, and better eating. Every meal is freshly prepared, hot, and nutritious.
01:00 PM – 02:00 PM
Arts & Enrichment
The afternoon is dedicated to the activities members love most — music, art, storytelling, building, and scrapbooking. These aren't just enjoyable; they're proven to improve mood and deepen social connection.
02:00 PM – 03:30 PM
Afternoon Programming
Gardening, cooking demonstrations, opportunities to volunteer, themed outings, or intergenerational visits. Every afternoon offers something to look forward to.
03:30 PM – 05:00 PM
Wind-Down & Departure
We close each day with something fun and calming: a musical sing-along or story sharing, followed by a gentle wind-down and a familiar transition home. Extended hours until 06:00 PM are available for families who need them.
