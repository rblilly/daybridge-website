About us

We saw what was missing, so we built it.

If you’re here, you already know how hard this is. A diagnosis that raises more questions than it answers. A healthcare system that diagnoses and manages but doesn’t support the daily reality of living with memory loss. The exhausting work of piecing everything together yourself, in a way that’s hard to describe to anyone who hasn’t lived it.

As operators and clinicians with decades of experience in primary and specialty care, we spent years sitting across from families in exactly your position.

We couldn’t stop asking ourselves why there wasn’t a place designed specifically for this journey.
Daybridge became the answer.

Our approach combines tailored programming, purposeful social engagement, and on-site clinical support to slow decline, lift mood, and reduce the agitation that wears down caregivers and their loved ones — giving both members and families better days.

Created specifically for adults living with memory loss and the families caring for them, Daybridge helps its members live safely, purposefully, and with greater independence for longer, while giving their families a trusted place to turn.
