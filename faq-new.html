Frequently asked questions

Your questions, answered.

Daybridge is designed for people living with memory loss — including Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia — who can benefit from a structured, supportive daytime environment. We welcome members at various stages of their journey, and our team works to tailor the experience to each person's needs and abilities.
Days include morning check-ins, enrichment activities, movement, a communal lunch, arts and creative programming, and an afternoon wind-down. See the full schedule on our How We Care page.
Daybridge is $180 per day, billed monthly based on attendance. There are no tiers or hidden fees. We're private pay and happy to help you explore coverage options that may apply.
