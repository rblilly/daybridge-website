How we care

Whole family care.

A day program built for people living with memory loss — and those who care for them.

Daytime connection

Familiar faces, consistent routines, and compassionate staff create days filled with joy, purpose, and real connection.

Small groups. Personalized programming that supports memory, mobility, and emotional well-being.

Therapy onsite. Physical, occupational, and mental health, prescribed by your provider.

1:6 care ratio. A safe, structured environment staffed by dementia-trained professionals.

A true clinical partner

Our interdisciplinary team pairs personalized daytime programming with clinical support that evolves as needs change.

Clinical oversight. RN- and MSW-led assessments, medication support, and care coordination.

Earlier visibility. Changes in cognition, mobility, mood, and function.

The right next step. Guidance before crises force harder decisions.

For caregivers, too

Designed to support the entire family, not just their loved one. We equip caregivers with the time, tools, and support they deserve.

Reliable respite. Time every week to work, rest, or simply breathe.

Support at home. Training and resources to strengthen care outside our center.

Never alone. Counseling and caregiver support groups, available at every step.

A day at Daybridge

Connection, joy, and purpose, every day.

With room for rest and spontaneity. Here's a glimpse of what members can expect.

08:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Arrival & Morning Check-In
Members arrive in small groups to ease the morning transition. Daily health screenings are completed upon arrival, while members settle in and enjoy coffee or tea together.
10:00 AM – 11:00 AM
Enrichment Activities
Cognitive activities like memory games and trivia are scheduled early in the day, when minds are freshest and alert. Members choose among options that match their interests. Our trained staff makes sure every activity is adapted to meet each member's needs.
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Movement
We prioritize movement that incorporates dual-task activities — exercises that engage mind and body. Our approach improves balance, reduces falls, and supports overall function. Activities include tai chi, ballroom dancing, walking conversations, and chair exercises, all adapted to each member's abilities.
12:00 PM – 01:00 PM
Lunch
Lunch is served communally, with space for small groups to break off. Shared meals naturally encourage conversation, connection, and better eating. Every meal is freshly prepared, hot, and nutritious.
01:00 PM – 02:00 PM
Arts & Enrichment
The afternoon is dedicated to the activities members love most — music, art, storytelling, building, and scrapbooking. These aren't just enjoyable; they're proven to improve mood and deepen social connection.
02:00 PM – 03:30 PM
Afternoon Programming
Gardening, cooking demonstrations, opportunities to volunteer, themed outings, or intergenerational visits. Every afternoon offers something to look forward to.
03:30 PM – 05:00 PM
Wind-Down & Departure
We close each day with something fun and calming: a musical sing-along or story sharing, followed by a gentle wind-down and a familiar transition home. Extended hours until 06:00 PM are available for families who need them.

Sample schedule, subject to change.
