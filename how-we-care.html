How we care

The Daybridge difference.

Every detail intentionally designed for people living with memory loss and the families who care for them.

Daytime connection

Familiar faces, consistent routines, and dementia-trained staff create days filled with joy, purpose, and real connection.

Tailored programming. Memory, movement, and emotional wellbeing through small-group experiences.

Nourishing meals. Freshly prepared hot lunch and healthy snacks, with a focus on hydration.

Consistent care. A 1:6 aide-to-member ratio means we know everyone by name.

More than a day program

Our onsite care team brings together nursing, social work, and therapy support, personalized for each member.

Medication support. Overseen by RNs, aligned with provider-prescribed care plans.

Therapy onsite. Physical, occupational, and mental health, prescribed by your provider.

Early detection. Daily check-ins, monthly vitals, and quarterly assessments to spot changes sooner.

For caregivers, too

Designed to support an entire family, not just the member. We equip caregivers with the time, tools, and support to navigate with confidence.

Reliable respite. Time every week to work, rest, or simply breathe.

Support for home. Training and resources to strengthen care outside our center.

Caregiver community. Counseling and support groups, available at every step.

A day at Daybridge

Connection, joy, and purpose, every day.

With room for rest and spontaneity. Here's a glimpse of what members can expect.

08:00 AM – 10:00 AM
Arrival & Morning Check-In
Members arrive in small groups to ease the morning transition. Daily health screenings are completed upon arrival, while members settle in and enjoy coffee or tea together.
10:00 AM – 11:00 AM
Enrichment Activities
Cognitive activities like memory games and trivia are scheduled early in the day, when minds are freshest. Members choose among options that match their interests, adapted to meet each member's needs.
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Movement
We prioritize movement that incorporates dual-task activities — exercises that engage mind and body. Activities include tai chi, ballroom dancing, walking conversations, and chair exercises, all adapted to each member's abilities.
12:00 PM – 01:00 PM
Lunch
Lunch is served communally, with space for small groups to break off. Shared meals naturally encourage conversation, connection, and better eating. Every meal is freshly prepared, hot, and nutritious.
01:00 PM – 02:00 PM
Arts & Enrichment
The afternoon is dedicated to the activities members love most — music, art, storytelling, building, and scrapbooking. These aren't just enjoyable; they're proven to improve mood and deepen social connection.
02:00 PM – 03:30 PM
Afternoon Programming
Gardening, cooking demonstrations, opportunities to volunteer, themed outings, or intergenerational visits. Every afternoon offers something to look forward to.
03:30 PM – 05:00 PM
Wind-Down & Departure
We close each day with something fun and calming: a musical sing-along or story sharing, followed by a gentle wind-down and a familiar transition home. Extended hours until 06:00 PM available for families who need them.
